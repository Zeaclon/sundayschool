Come Follow Me


December 1–7: “The Vision of the Redemption of the Dead”


Scriptures: Doctrine and Covenants 137–138

The revelations recorded in Doctrine and Covenants 137 and 138 answer questions about life after death. Section 137 was received by Joseph Smith in 1836; Section 138 was received by Joseph F. Smith in 1918. Together they testify of “the great and wonderful love made manifest by the Father and the Son” (D&C 138:3).
