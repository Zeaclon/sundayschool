Class Discussion: The Vision of the Redemption of the Dead

Based on D&C 137–138, here are some topics and questions to consider in class.

Understanding the Vision

  • What did Joseph Smith learn about his brother Alvin?
  • How does D&C 138 expand on what we know about the spirit world?

Applying the Teachings

  • How can these revelations strengthen our faith?
  • Why is it important that the Savior’s work continues after death?

Personal Reflection

  • What questions do you still have about life after death?
  • How can you apply these teachings in your study or teaching?
